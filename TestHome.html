About this Site

What we do, create, and promise influences the perception of Grand Valley.

Expressing a unified identity—in look, tone, and consistency—relies on our campus communicators. By incorporating the information from this site into your work, you play a vital role in advancing Grand Valley's identity. apply to all GVSU colleges, departments, centers, institutes, and non-athletic administrative offices.

Need some help?

There's a lot here, we know. But a strong, consistent, powerful brand benefits everyone at Grand Valley. We're here to help you, too. Institutional Marketing can answer your questions and offer advice on marketing strategy, design, writing, and websites.
